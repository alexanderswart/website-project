Alex's Trip to Ireland

November 2022

For Fall Break, I visited Dublin, Ireland. I had been to Ireland before back in 2017, but it was an incredible experience to revisit the country and all it's natural beauty.

Some of the highlights of my trip included: a trip to the Cliffs of Moher, visiting Galway along the coast, learning how to pour the perfect pint of Guinness, and touring the Jameson factory.
